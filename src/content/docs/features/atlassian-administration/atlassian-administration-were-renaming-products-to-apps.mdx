## Description

At Team ‘25, we announced a shift in how we think about and refer to our suite of individual products, now called ‘Atlassian apps’, or ‘apps’ for short. The way you manage Atlassian apps like Jira and Confluence in Atlassian Administration remains unchanged. Read more about this change on Atlassian Community
