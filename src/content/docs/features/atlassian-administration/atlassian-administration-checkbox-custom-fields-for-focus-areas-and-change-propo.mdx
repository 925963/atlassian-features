## Description

Standardize your data and track simple yes or no information by adding checkbox custom fields to your Focus Areas and Change Proposals. This new field type in the Focus app allows you to model data more accurately to fit your business processes and workflows. To get started with this change: Go to Atlassian Administration, then select your Focus workspace.
