## Description

Earlier this year, we introduced Managed Teams as a new type of team that is synced to a group with the caveat that the team would have to share the name of the group. In June, the team names could be changed from the Team profile but with this release, we've made it so managed teams can now be renamed in Atlassian Administration as well.
