## Description

We have updated the experience for deactivating AI in your administrator settings. This change introduces a new flow that makes it clearer and easier to manage AI deactivation for your organization. To try the new AI deactivation flow: Go to your administrator settings.
	Find the section for AI features.
	Select Deactivate AI and follow the updated steps.
