## Description

Site admins can now filter audit log activities by Jira and Confluence in Atlassian Administration. This update ensures that all admins have a consistent experience when tracking activities across their organization, making it easier to find and review specific app-related events. To get started with this change: Go to Atlassian Administration.
	From your Atlassian site, select Settings, then Audit log.
	Select the App filter dropdown.
	Choose Jira or Confluence to view activities for those apps
