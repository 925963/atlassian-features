## Description

If you paste a large amount of text into an empty Confluence page or Live Doc, a summarized title will automatically be generated for you. To try out the new AI-generated title feature: Open an empty Confluence page or Live Doc.
	Paste a large block of text (more than 50 characters).
	A summarized title will be automatically generated based on your content.
