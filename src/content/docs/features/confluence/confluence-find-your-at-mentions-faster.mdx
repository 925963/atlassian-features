## Description

Stay on top of the conversations that matter most. You can now filter the comments panel in Confluence to surface only the comments where you’ve been mentioned. This gives you a focused view of the discussions that require your attention, whether you were mentioned in the original comment or a reply. Open the Comments panel on any page.
	Select the filter menu at the top of the panel.
	Select Mentions me to filter for your mentions.
