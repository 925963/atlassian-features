## Description

Back in March 2025, we released Managed Teams for Premium and Enterprise customers. This allowed organization admins to sync an Atlassian team to a group to better control team information and membership, but this change meant the team name would change to match the synced group. Now, organization admins can change a managed team's name from the team profile and while staying synced to the group. To change a managed team's name: Go to the managed team's profile.
