## Description

Stay up to date with important Confluence activity. Mobile users will now receive Recap push notifications to help them catch up on relevant updates they may have missed. To turn on Recap push notifications: Navigate to Settings, then Push notifications.
	Turn on the Recap toggle to enable notifications.
