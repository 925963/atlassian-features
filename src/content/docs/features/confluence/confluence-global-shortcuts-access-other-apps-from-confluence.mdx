## Description

We’ve improved how you navigate between your Atlassian apps by adding global shortcuts to the primary navigation in Confluence. You can now quickly find and open other apps like Jira, Loom, and Goals directly from your sidebar, making it easier to switch between tools and stay productive. Open Confluence.
	Locate the primary navigation sidebar.
	Find the list of app shortcuts at the bottom of the sidebar.
	Select an app to open it in a new tab, or select More to see additional options.
