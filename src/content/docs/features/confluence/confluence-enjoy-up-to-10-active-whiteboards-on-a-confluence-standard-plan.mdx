## Description

Confluence Standard now allows you to have up to 10 active, editable whiteboards. To take advantage of the new whiteboard limit in Confluence Standard: Open Confluence and navigate to your workspace.
	Create or edit whiteboards as needed. You can now have up to 10 active, editable whiteboards. Additional whiteboards will then be view-only.
