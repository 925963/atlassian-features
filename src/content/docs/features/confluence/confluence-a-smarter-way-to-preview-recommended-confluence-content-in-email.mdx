## Description

We are testing a new experience where some people will see an AI-generated summary of recommended Confluence content in their email, instead of the usual first 200 characters. This aims to make it easier to understand what the content is about at a glance. To see if you are part of this experiment and view the new AI-generated summaries in your recommended Confluence content emails: Check your inbox for emails recommending Confluence content.
