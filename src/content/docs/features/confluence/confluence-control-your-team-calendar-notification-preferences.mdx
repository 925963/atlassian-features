## Description

You can now manage your Team Calendar notification preferences directly from your email settings. This gives you more control over which Team Calendar emails you receive. To update your Team Calendar notification preferences: Go to your account’s email settings page.
	Find the section for Team Calendar notifications.
	Choose to opt in or out of Team Calendar notification emails as you prefer.
	Save your changes.
