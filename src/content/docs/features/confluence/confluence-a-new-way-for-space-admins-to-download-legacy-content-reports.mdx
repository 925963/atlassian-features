## Description

Confluence space admins can now download a CSV report listing all legacy pages and templates in their space. This makes it easier to see how many legacy pages and templates exist in the space, which ones are eligible for conversion, and which ones contain unsupported content in order to enable planning to transition from tinyMCE to fabric editor.
