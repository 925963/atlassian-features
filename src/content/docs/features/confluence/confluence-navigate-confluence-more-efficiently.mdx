## Description

We've updated Confluence's navigation to make it simpler and more intuitive to use. Now, the top navigation is reserved for essential tasks and global settings, and everything related to the work you do with your team is in the sidebar. We've also made the sidebar more content-centric, moving advanced features out of the way so you can focus on collaboration.
