## Description

The CSV export experience in space settings has been updated with a modern look and feel. You will notice stylistic improvements to the Export Space area, but the export to CSV functionality remains unchanged. To use the updated CSV export experience: Go to your Confluence space.
	Select "Space settings" from the sidebar.
	Choose "Export Space".
	Select the "CSV" export option to see the refreshed interface.
