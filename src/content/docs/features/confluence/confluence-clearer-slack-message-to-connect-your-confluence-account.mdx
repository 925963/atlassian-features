## Description

When a Confluence link is shared in a Slack channel, you’ll see a clearer message that helps you connect or log in to your Confluence account. This makes it easier to turn on real-time Confluence notifications in Slack and stay connected to your team’s work. Make sure the Confluence Slack app is installed in your Slack workspace.
	Share or receive a Confluence link in any Slack channel. This doesn’t include direct messages.
