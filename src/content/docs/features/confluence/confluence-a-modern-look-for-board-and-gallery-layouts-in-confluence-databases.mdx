## Description

We have updated the visuals for Board and Gallery (previously Card) layouts in Confluence Databases to provide a more modern, smooth, and clean experience. To see the new visuals in Confluence Databases: Open your Confluence workspace.
	Navigate to a database and select either the Board or Gallery layout.
	View your content in the updated layout to experience the new look and feel.
