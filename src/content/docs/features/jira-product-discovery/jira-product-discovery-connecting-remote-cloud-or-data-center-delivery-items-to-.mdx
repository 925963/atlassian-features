## Description

You can now connect Jira Product Discovery ideas with delivery items on remote Cloud or Data Center instances linked to your Jira Product Discovery site. After setup, a new Site field appears, letting you choose whether to create or link a delivery item locally or remotely. This integration keeps delivery progress in sync across your Cloud sites and Data Center instances, so you no longer need to update items manually on each platform.
