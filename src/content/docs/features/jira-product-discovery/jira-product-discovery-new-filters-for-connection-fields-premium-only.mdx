## Description

Advanced filters are now available for connection fields in Jira Product Discovery views. When you add connection fields to your view, a dedicated Connections section appears in the Filters panel with Match group and Match column filters that apply to all connection fields simultaneously, streamlining complex filtering scenarios. When you group your view or organize it by columns using connection fields, additional filtering controls become available in the corresponding Group by or Columns pane
