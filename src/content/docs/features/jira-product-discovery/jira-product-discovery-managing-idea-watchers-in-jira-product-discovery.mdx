## Description

Users with the default Administrator and Creator roles can now add and remove watchers for ideas in Jira Product Discovery. This helps you control who receives notifications about idea updates. To manage watchers: Go to your Jira Product Discovery space.
	Open the needed idea.
	At the top of the right panel, select Watch options.
	Under Watching this work item, select the cross icon next to the user's name to remove that user.
