## Description

You can now connect ideas from all spaces on your site using the connection field in Jira Product Discovery. Configure the field settings and add related ideas from any space to your views. This improvement helps to maintain better visibility of related work across your entire organization. To connect different types of ideas: In your Jira Product Discovery view, select Fields.
	In the right panel, find the needed connection field, then select Edit field next to it.
