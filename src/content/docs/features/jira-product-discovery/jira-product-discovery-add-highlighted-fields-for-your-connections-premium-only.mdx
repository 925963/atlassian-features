## Description

You can now add highlighted fields for connections directly from the Fields settings in Jira Product Discovery views. To display additional field values alongside your connections, choose the field to highlight based on the most useful data for your team. To add a highlighted field: In your Jira Product Discovery view, select Fields.
	In the right panel, find the needed connection field, then select Edit field next to it.
	From the Highlighted field dropdown, select a field whose values you want
