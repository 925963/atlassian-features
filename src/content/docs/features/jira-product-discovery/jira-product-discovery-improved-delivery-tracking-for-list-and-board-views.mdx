## Description

Updated visual cues for the delivery progress and delivery status fields provide more delivery-tracking information in the list and board views in Jira Product Discovery. The delivery progress field now displays both a progress bar and a percentage, regardless of whether it’s set to track work items or story points. Hover over either field to open a card with more details about the work item or story point count, completion percentage, and quick actions to link or create Jira work items. Discove
