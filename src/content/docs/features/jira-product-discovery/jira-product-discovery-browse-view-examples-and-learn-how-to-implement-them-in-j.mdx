## Description

You can now discover the view examples directly within your Jira Product Discovery space. These examples cover various use cases such as scoring and prioritizing ideas, creating quarterly roadmaps, and tracking Jira delivery progress. Browse and preview these examples to quickly identify approaches that fit your workflow. To get the view example: Go to your Jira Product Discovery.
	Next to the space name, select +.
	Under Explore examples, select any view from the list to open its image.
