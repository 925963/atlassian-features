## Description

In the About section of your focus area, you can quickly record a Loom video that digs deeper into the details and adds that personal touch. If you already recorded one, you can also include that link. Loom videos can also be recorded when crafting an update that shares progress with your followers. Links to previously recorded Loom videos can be added there too.
