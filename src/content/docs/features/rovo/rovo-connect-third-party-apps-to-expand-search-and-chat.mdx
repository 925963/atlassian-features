## Description

Eligible users in Confluence and Jira can now connect third-party apps through Rovo, making it easier to search and chat across more content and conversations. This update helps you get more relevant results and insights in Rovo Search and Chat. To get started with this change: Sign in to Confluence or Jira.
	When prompted, select the option to connect third-party apps.
	Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the connection.
