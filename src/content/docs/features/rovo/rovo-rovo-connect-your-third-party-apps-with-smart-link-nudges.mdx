## Description

Improve your search results and AI responses in Atlassian Rovo by connecting your third-party apps. When you paste a smart link from an app you haven't authenticated yet into a Confluence page, Rovo will now provide a helpful nudge to help you connect that account. To get started with this change: Paste a link from a third-party app (such as Google Drive, Slack, or GitHub) into a Confluence page.
