## Description

We are introducing a new, refreshed look for Rovo Chat. This update brings a modern design and improved experience to your conversations. To experience the new Rovo Chat design: Open Rovo Chat in your Atlassian app.
	If you do not see the new look, ensure your workspace is included in the rollout or check with your administrator.
	Start a conversation or continue an existing one to explore the updated design.
