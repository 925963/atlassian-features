## Description

Rovo Chat and Agents now offers more granular control over your knowledge sources by replacing the deprecated Atlas filter with separate options for Projects and Goals. This change allows you to refine Rovo Chat and Rovo Agent responses to focus specifically on the high-level outcomes or the detailed work tracking you need, ensuring more relevant and accurate information retrieval across your Atlassian apps. To get started with this change: Open Rovo Chat.
