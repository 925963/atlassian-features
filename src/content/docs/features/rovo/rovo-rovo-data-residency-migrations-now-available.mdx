## Description

Organization admins can now move and pin their Atlassian Rovo data to specific geographical locations. This update for Rovo allows you to meet your organization's data residency requirements by managing where your in-scope app data is hosted. To get started with this change: Log in to admin.atlassian.com.
	Go to Security, then Data residency.
	Select Move data and follow the prompts to migrate your Rovo data to your preferred location.
