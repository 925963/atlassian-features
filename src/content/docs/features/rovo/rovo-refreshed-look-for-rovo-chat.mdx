## Description

We are introducing a new, refreshed look for Rovo Chat. This update brings a modern visual style to your chat experience, making it easier and more enjoyable to communicate and collaborate. To see the new look in Rovo Chat: Open Rovo Chat from your usual location in Confluence or Jira.
	Start a conversation or join an existing chat to experience the updated design.
