## Description

A new onboarding mini-modal for Rovo Chat introduces a guided experience to help you get started with Chat. The mini-modal matches the look and feel of Rovo Chat and encourages you to try out its features. To get started with this change: Navigate to your Jira site, and navigate to any board.
	After a few seconds, the onboarding mini-modal will show up. Follow the prompts in it to explore Chat features.
