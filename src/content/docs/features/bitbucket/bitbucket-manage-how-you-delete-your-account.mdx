## Description

When you chose to delete your Bitbucket account, you'll be redirected to your account preferences to complete the process. To delete your Bitbucket account: Select Personal Bitbucket settings then Account settings.
	Select Delete account. You'll be redirected to Atlassian account preferences to confirm your change.
