## Description

You can now introduce a final step that always executes at the end of your pipeline, even if previous steps fail or are skipped. This ensures that critical post-pipeline activities like resource cleanup, log collection, and notifications are completed reliably every time. To add a final step to your pipelines: Open your bitbucket-pipelines.yml file.
	Add a final step at the end of your pipeline configuration
	Define the script and properties you want to run as the last action.
	Commit and push y
