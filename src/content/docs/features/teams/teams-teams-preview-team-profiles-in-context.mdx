## Description

We've integrated a new preview panel into the Teams app to help you stay in flow while browsing. Now, when you select a team from the directory or a user profile, a preview panel opens on the right instead of navigating you to a new tab. This change provides a consistent experience across Atlassian apps and makes it easier to explore teams without losing your place. To get started with this change Open the Teams app.
