## Description

Organization admins can now delegate team management permissions to other users based on team types. This change allows users who aren't organization admins to create, manage, or delete teams of a specific type in the Teams app, helping to distribute administrative tasks and improve team autonomy. To get started with this change: Go to Team Settings in the Teams app.
	Select Team types.
	Select Edit for the team type you want to manage.
	Grant the Create teams, Manage teams, or Delete teams perm
