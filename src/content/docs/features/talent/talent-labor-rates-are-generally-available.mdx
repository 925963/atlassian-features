## Description

We’re thrilled to announce that the first milestone of Funds in Strategy Collection is generally available today: Labor Rates in Talent and Focus. By setting up your labor rate cards in Atlassian Talent, you can track annualized costs for your focus areas and your organization, and unlock a live view of your financial reality. Read more about the Strategy Collection.
