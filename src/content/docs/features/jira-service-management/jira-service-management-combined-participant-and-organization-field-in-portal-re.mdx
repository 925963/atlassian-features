## Description

Jira Service Management now combines the participant and organization fields into a single field on portal request forms, making it easier to share requests with individuals and organizations. This streamlines the sharing process for customers and agents. To get started with this change: Open a portal request form in Jira Service Management.
	Look for the new combined field to share the request with participants or organizations.
