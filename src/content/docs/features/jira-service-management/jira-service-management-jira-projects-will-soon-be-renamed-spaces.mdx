## Description

As more teams make Jira their home for work, we’re committed to improving the language in our tools to better reflect the diverse ways you define and manage your work. As part of these efforts, the term project will soon be replaced with space across all Jira Cloud products, including Jira Service Management and Jira Product Discovery. • Read more about these changes on the Atlassian Community
