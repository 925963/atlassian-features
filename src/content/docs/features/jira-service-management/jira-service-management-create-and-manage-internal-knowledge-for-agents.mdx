## Description

You can now create and manage internal knowledge directly in your space, giving your agents the ability to access content specific to your team's need. To create and manage internal knowledge for your agents: In your space settings, select Knowledge base.
	Select the Internal tab then Add knowledge to add new internal knowledge for your team.
