## Description

You can now easily set up operations from the Your work page in Jira Service Management if you haven't set them up yet. With operations, you can empower your team to stay on top of alerts with advanced alerting and on-call capabilities. * Integrate your apps and keep alert data in sync 
* Route alerts to the right people
