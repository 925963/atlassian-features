## Description

You can now configure your queue from the same space you manage other space-level settings. This makes it easier to keep your queues aligned with how your team works, without switching between separate queue views. To manage queues in your space: Go to Space settings, then select Request management, then Queues.
	Review and configure your queues as needed.
