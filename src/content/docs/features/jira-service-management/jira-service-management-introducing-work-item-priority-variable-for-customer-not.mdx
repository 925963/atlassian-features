## Description

We're adding a new variable called work item priority to customer notifications. You can add this variable to your notification templates to insert the priority of a work item in your message. To add variables to your notification templates for customers: From your service project, select Project settings, then Notifications, then Customer notifications.
	Choose the notification you want to edit, then select Edit.
