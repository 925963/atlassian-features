## Description

New Jira Service Management admins will only see the most important setup tasks when getting started, making onboarding less overwhelming. To onboard to Jira Service Management as a new admin: Sign in to your Jira Service Management as an admin.
	Go to the Get Started section from your project (soon to be 'space') sidebar.
