## Description

You can now add teammates to your Jira Service Management project right from the project sidebar. For consistency, this new invite modal is the same experience as in Space settings under People & Access. To give this a try: Open your Jira Service Management project.
	In the project sidebar, select Add people.
	Use the new invite modal to add teammates by following the prompts.
