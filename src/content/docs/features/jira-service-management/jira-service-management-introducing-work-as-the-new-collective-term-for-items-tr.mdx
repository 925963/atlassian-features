## Description

As more teams make Jira their home for work, we’re committed to improving the language in our tools to better reflect the diverse ways you define and manage your work. We’re introducing work as the new collective term for all items tracked in Jira. We’re also exploring ways to incorporate the language you use for your own work types. • Read more about these changes on the Atlassian Community.
