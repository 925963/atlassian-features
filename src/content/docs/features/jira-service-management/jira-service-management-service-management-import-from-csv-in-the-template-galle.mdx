## Description

Jira Service Management now includes an Import into JSM (CSV) option directly within the Service management and Jira Service Management template gallery. This update allows teams to quickly set up a realistic project by importing historical tickets, users, and configurations from other service management tools. To get started with this change: Go to the Service management or Jira Service Management category in the template gallery.
	Select Import into JSM (CSV).
