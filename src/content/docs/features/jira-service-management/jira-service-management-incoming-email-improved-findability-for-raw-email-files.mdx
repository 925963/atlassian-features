## Description

Admins can now more easily find and download the raw incoming email file from Jira Service Management incoming email logs for faster troubleshooting. This update improves the visibility of an existing action only and does not change how raw email downloads work. To get started with this change: Go to Space settings → Channels and self service → Email.
	Click View logs for the relevant email channel.
	Use Download raw email message to retrieve the raw email file.
