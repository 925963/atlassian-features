## Description

As the assignee of work items that are overdue or due today, you'll now receive daily notifications within Jira and via email. Each notification links to a pre-filtered page showing your overdue and due work items. Manage your due date notification preferences by: 1. Select Settings. 2. Select Personal Jira Settings, then Digests.
