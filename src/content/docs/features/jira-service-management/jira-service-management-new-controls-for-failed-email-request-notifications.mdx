## Description

We’ve introduced new settings to give you more control over notifications for failed email requests. By default, notifications are sent to all listed admins of a project. With the new controls, you can select to send notifications to a specific email address or even turn them off entirely. In addition, we’ve introduced a new setting that lets you decide whether customers are informed when their requests fail due to access restrictions or self-signup being disabled.
