## Description

You can now forward work items directly within Jira Customer Service Management. This feature streamlines the process for teams to flag and prioritize work items that need developer attention, making it easier to manage escalations without leaving the app. To forward a work item in Jira Customer Service Management: Open a work item in Jira Customer Service Management.
	Look for the new Forward option in the work item actions.
	Select Forward and follow the prompts to complete the escalation.
