## Description

This change allows users to bypass approvals while preserving approval data, preventing data loss and improving history tracking. During an approval state of a workflow, an issue can't transition to the approved or declined status until all approval decisions are finalized. However, transitions other than ‘Approve’ or ‘Decline’ can be used to manually transition issues and bypass the approval state.
