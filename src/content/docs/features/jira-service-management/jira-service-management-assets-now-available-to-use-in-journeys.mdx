## Description

You can now set Assets to fields on work items in journeys to further power your processes. This allows you to better manage large-scale processes such as Onboarding or Offboarding where Assets data plays a crucial role. For example, in an onboarding journey you can use Assets to auto-assign specific laptops or register for specific trainings based on your role.
