## Description

Space administrators can now automatically archive inactive incident Slack channels and control whether responder team members are added to incident Slack channels or Microsoft Teams chat in Jira Service Management. These features help keep your workspace organized and reduce unnecessary notifications for your team. (Premium & Enterprise only) To get started with this change: In your Premium or Enterprise service space, go to Space settings, then Incident management.
