## Description

You can now filter requests on the My requests page in Jira Service Management by request title, request status, or owner. This makes it easier to find and manage your requests in the customer portal. To get started with this change: Go to the customer portal in Jira Service Management.
	Open the My requests page.
	Use the filter options to narrow requests by title, status, or owner.
