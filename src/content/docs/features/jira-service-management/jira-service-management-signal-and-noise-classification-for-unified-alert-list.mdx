## Description

Jira Service Management Premium and Enterprise customers with Atlassian Intelligence enabled can now see alerts and alert groups classified as signal or noise in the unified alert list. This helps teams focus on the most important incidents and reduce alert fatigue. To get started with this change: In Jira Service Management, open the unified alert list.
