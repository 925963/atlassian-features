## Description

We’re introducing a new Assets navigation component for customers on the Free plan. This update provides a dedicated entry point that takes you directly to the Assets landing page, where you can learn how to manage objects and explore the benefits of asset tracking within your Atlassian app. To view the Assets landing page: Click on the Assets component.
	Explore the landing page to see how to organize and track your resources.
