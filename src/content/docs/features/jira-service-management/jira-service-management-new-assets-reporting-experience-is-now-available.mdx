## Description

Now with greater flexibility and options to customize how you visualize your objects, attributes, and associated work items, the new assets dashboard experience improves data transparency, helps teams anticipate maintenance needs, and drives data-driven decisions for future investments. To try the new assets dashboard experience: Select Assets in your service management project.
