## Description

In support of Atlassian’s System of Work and our evolution toward a unified Atlassian platform, we’re shifting how we think about and refer to our suite of individual products, now called ‘Atlassian apps’, or ‘apps’ for short. This is a naming update only and won’t affect how you use the apps.
