## Description

The Loom onboarding experience in Jira and Confluence now guides eligible first-time users through only the essential steps, making it easier to grant camera and microphone permissions and start recording. This update helps users complete their first recording faster and with less confusion. To get started with this change: Open Loom from Jira or Confluence.
	Follow the new onboarding prompts to grant camera and microphone permissions and begin your first recording.
