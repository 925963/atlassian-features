## Description

Users can now create super reactions—larger, more exciting emojis—directly on the video player. This feature enhances engagement and makes interactions more expressive for everyone watching videos. To get started with this change: Open a video in the video player.
	Select the emoji reaction option.
	Follow the prompts to press and hold the emoji to display a larger, animated emoji on the video.
