## Description

Loom has improved how video summaries appear when sharing links in Slack. By updating the summary block in the Slack unfurl experience, you can now see more relevant information at a glance, making it easier for your team to understand the context of a video without leaving the conversation. To get started with this change Paste a Loom video link into any Slack channel or direct message.
