## Description

Loom now sends notifications when someone reacts to your comment or to comments on your videos. This update helps creators and commenters stay informed about engagement on their videos, with notifications delivered via Loom, Slack, and mobile. Email notifications are not sent for reactions to comments. To get started with this change: React to a comment on a Loom video.Check your Loom notifications, Slack, or mobile app to see notifications for reactions to your comments or comments on your vide
