## Description

You can now see all workflows on your board and keep scrolling to load more work items on business space boards. We've also added new date-based filters, improved controls for hiding done work items, and refreshed the actions menu to make it easier to manage your work. For existing boards that use multiple workflows, admins will be prompted to transition current workflow-specific board settings into multiple board views.
