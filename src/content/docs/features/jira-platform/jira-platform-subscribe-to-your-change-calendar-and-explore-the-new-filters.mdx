## Description

We’ve made major improvements to the change calendar to help you stay on top of change events and avoid conflicts across your services, Assets objects, and teams. Subscribe to change calendar from your external calendar You can now generate an iCal link and subscribe to your change calendar from apps like Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, and Outlook.
