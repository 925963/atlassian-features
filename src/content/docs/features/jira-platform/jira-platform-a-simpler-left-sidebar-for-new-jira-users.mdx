## Description

We're simplifying the left sidebar for people who are new to Jira. This update reduces the number of items shown in the sidebar, making it easier to get started and find what you need. To see the simplified sidebar: Sign in to Jira as a new customer.
	Look at the left side of your screen to view the updated sidebar.
	Explore the available options to start your work.
