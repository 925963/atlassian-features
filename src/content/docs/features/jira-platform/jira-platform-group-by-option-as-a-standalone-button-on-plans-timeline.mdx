## Description

The group by option on the Jira Plans timeline is now available as a dedicated, standalone button. This update makes it easier to find and use the group by feature, helping you organize your timeline more efficiently. Open your Jira Plans timeline.
	Select the new Group by button to organize your timeline by the available options.
