## Description

The calendar view in Jira software projects and business projects now has a Week view. The calendar will remember your selected view, making it easy to return to your preferred setup. To use the new week view in your calendar: Open your project in Jira.
	Select Calendar from the navigation menu.
	Choose the Week option to display your work items in a weekly grid format.
	Your selected view range will be saved in the URL for easy access next time.
