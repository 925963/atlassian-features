## Description

The new list view (previously 'All work' in Jira) can now be sorted manually with drag-and-drop. This update lets you drag-and-drop to reorder items in the list, making it easier to prioritize and organize work. You can still sort by any field; drag-and-drop will be temporarily disabled while sorting is applied. To return to manual ordering, you can either clear your applied sorting or clear your filters to re-enable drag-and-drop and move work items up or down the list.
