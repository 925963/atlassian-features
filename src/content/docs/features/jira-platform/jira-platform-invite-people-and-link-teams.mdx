## Description

Invite teammates to your Jira projects more quickly with a new, prominent button in your project header. Jira now features an Invite people button directly on board and list views, replacing the previous menu-based navigation to help you bring collaborators into your work without searching through settings. To get started with this change: Open a board or list view in any Jira company-managed or team-managed project.
