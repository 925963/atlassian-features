## Description

You can now add people to a specific project when you invite them to join Jira. This means you can land your teammates in the right place as soon as they join Jira, and they can get started without needing to track down a specific project first. To add someone to a project: Select Teams from the sidebar to open the Teams app.
	Select People.
	Select Add people.
