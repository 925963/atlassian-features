## Description

We've added four business space templates, helping teams quickly start work with layouts tailored to their needs. The new templates are the Marketing Team template, Design Team template, Customer Projects template, and Social Media Planner template. To get started with this change: Go to Jira Cloud.
	Start creating a new business space.
	Open the template selector.
	Browse the business categories to view and select the new templates.
