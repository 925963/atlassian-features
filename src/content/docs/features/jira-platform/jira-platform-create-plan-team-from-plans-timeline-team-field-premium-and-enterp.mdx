## Description

This update to Jira lets you create a plan team directly from the team field in the Plans timeline, making it easier to organize and manage your teams within Jira. This improvement streamlines team setup and helps you keep your planning process efficient. To get started with this change: Open the Plans timeline in Jira.
	Select the team field where you want to create a new plan team.
	Follow the prompts to create and configure your plan team.
