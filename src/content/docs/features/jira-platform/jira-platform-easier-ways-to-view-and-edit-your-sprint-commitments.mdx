## Description

This change makes it easier for you to spot and adjust sprint commitments in your backlog. Now you can: Quickly understand if the sprint is over or undercommitted, with new icons and recommended improvements. The up arrow shows if the sprint is overcommitted, the down arrow shows it's undercommitted, and the tick indicates it’s just right!
